Identificación del problema y análisis de requerimientos

 

Caso de Estudio : COP 16

 

Cliente

Alcaldía de Cali y COP 16

 

Usuario

COP 16 participants, inhabitants of biodiversity areas, and the public in general

Contexto del problema

 

[The mayor's office of Cali needs a software that allows registering, consulting, and modifying information related to places with biological diversity, in addition, registering communities that can be associated with the places, users must be able to register species to a place, as well as modify them, the users must be able to register products associated to a community and delete them as well, it is necessary to be able to consult the information of a specific place, consult the information of communities that are in a specific department and see the information of places with a certain type of problems, added to this the place with the greatest number of species and the three largest places, all this for the creation and navigability of all the elements that constitute the environment of Colombia, central topic in the COP 16]

 

Requerimientos funcionales

RF1 Place registration

RF2 Community registration

RF3 Registration of a species in a place

RF4 Registration of a product in a community

RF5 Delete a product of a community

RF6 Modify the information of a species in a place

RF7 Consult the information of a place

RF8 Consult communities in  a department

RF9 Consult the information of the communities whose problems are hospital or school absence

RF10 Consult the place with the largest number of species

RF11 Consult the three largest places

RF12 Create proof cases

Requerimientos no funcionales

RNF1 The program must be easy to use

RNF2 The program must done in 3 weeks

RNF3 The program needs to be strongly safe to save all the information

RFN4 The program must be easy to maintain

RFN5 The program must be created with good practices

RFN6 The program must be documented

 

 

Identificador y nombre

[RF1 Place registration]

Resumen

(The system must save the name of the place and validate that it didn’t already exist, the department that is an enumeration, the area of the place in square kilometers, the type that is an enumeration, the URL of the photo, the economic resources needed, the name of the community that take care of it that must exist in the system, and the values of the date,all of that in an arrangement of type Place that must be initialized and validate if the registration is completely done)
